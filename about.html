About Me

My name is Júlio César Bernardo Leite, and I am a 20-year-old residing in Cornélio Procópio, Paraná. Currently, I am pursuing my studies in Software Engineering (5/8) at the Universidade Tecnológica Federal do Paraná.

I embarked on a transformative journey, spanning over 3000 kilometers, in search of knowledge and new experiences. Two years ago, I resided comfortably in my parents' home, content with the familiar. However, an epiphany struck me, igniting a deep longing for change within me. From a young age, I had an affinity for computers, though my interactions were merely playful and not deeply profound. I would disassemble notebooks, feigning expertise, and pretend to be a programmer without truly comprehending a single line of code. Upon completing high school, the weight of choosing a college path pressed upon me. Guided by my intuition, albeit without a clear understanding of my own actions, I gravitated towards a course that had always been silently present in my life. Little did I know, this choice would shape my future in profound ways.
